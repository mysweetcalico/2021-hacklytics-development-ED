Challenges
Healthcare
Data science is playing an important role in healthcare. Data analytic models can help healthcare providers make diagnostics faster and more accurately. In this track, we encourage hackers to design a solution or products to assist in overcoming the challenges in the healthcare industry.

Finance
The prevelence and importance of data science in finance is well-known. Could we use historical data to predict the next econmic recession? Could we use the data analytic tools to improve the financial security of our communities? Hacklytics 2021 encourage hackers to dive deep in data sets and produce a solution to a finance-related problems that you are most passionate about.

Sports
Statistics and quantitative analysis have played a centrol role in sports for a long time. The rapid development of data science topics like AI and ML means that it is totally possible to model your opponents' defense and optimize your attack strategies in a game. The challenge here is simple: design a solution that can have an impact on your beloved sport fields.
